# Wandering Through the Pines

There is something transformative about being surrounded by ancient trees. Last week, I spent three days hiking through the deep forests of the Cascades.

The silence there isn't actually silent—it's filled with the rustle of needles, the distant call of a hawk, and the steady heartbeat of the earth.

### The Gear
I'm traveling light these days. Just a small pack, a notebook, and a camera.
